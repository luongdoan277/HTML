Payment Guide

Payment on Delivery (COD)

We support payment service upon delivery. This service fee is US $ 1.8 / 41,000 VND. If you want to choose this service, you need to fill in the right phone number during the ordering process.

Pay By Paypal

When you pay with Paypal, your page will go to the Paypal payment page, where you can use your Paypal username and password to log in to your PayPal account to pay. If you do not have a Paypal account, you have payment. Please click "Pay by Debit Card / Credit Card" and you will be redirected to a secure page where you can enter your credit card information or use secure Paypal to pay.

Pay by Credit / Debit Card

Available credit card options are listed above. The credit card companies listed above are the most commonly used credit cards on this site. Don't worry if your credit card company isn't listed, we encourage you to go ahead and make a purchase. Please note that SHEIN does not collect your credit / debit card number or personal information when you make a payment. If you have questions about your transactions on our website, please consult the card issuing bank for information.

"Discount Code" And "Points"

You can use coupons and points at checkout. After entering your discount code and points, click "Use" and the system will automatically adjust the total amount. Please note that if you do not click "Use" before finalization, your total amount will not reflect offers from coupons and points.

Note:
  • Please make sure the discount code you enter is exactly the same as we give you. No spaces before, coincide or after. To avoid mistakes, we recommend that you copy / paste that code.
  • Cannot combine coupons. Each order has only one coupon.
  • Discount coupons will apply. This eliminates some products that are not available for coupons.
  • You can pay up to 70% of your purchase with reward points at checkout. Remember 100 points = 1 dollar.
